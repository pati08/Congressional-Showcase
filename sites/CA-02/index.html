The App

How It Works

Seen is a mental health app designed specifically for teenagers. It combines daily mood tracking with private journaling and the clinically validated PHQ-9 depression screening tool, all enhanced with AI-powered analysis.

The AI component analyzes mood patterns and journal entries to provide personalized insights, helping teens understand their emotional well-being. When the PHQ-9 assessment indicates concern, the app provides appropriate resources and encourages users to seek professional support.
