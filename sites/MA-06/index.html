The Platform

How It Works

Bridges is a bilingual web application that connects the communities of Lawrence and Andover, Massachusetts. The platform provides a shared space where residents from both towns can discover local events, access community resources, and engage with each other.

With full English and Spanish language support, Bridges ensures that language is never a barrier to community participation. The app aggregates local events, social services, and organizational resources into one accessible platform, fostering connections that cross both language and geographic boundaries.
