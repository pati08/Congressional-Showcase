The App

How It Works

Boat Knots is an educational app that teaches users how to tie essential nautical knots through a combination of high-quality photos and video demonstrations. Each knot includes step-by-step instructions with visual aids.

The app covers a variety of knots used in boating, from basic bowlines and cleat hitches to more advanced splices. It serves as both a learning tool for beginners and a quick reference guide for experienced boaters out on the water.
