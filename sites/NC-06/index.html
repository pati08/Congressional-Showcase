The App

How It Works

Nestling is an AI-powered copilot designed specifically for NICU parents. The app helps parents track their baby's growth metrics, understand medical records and lab results, and navigate the complex journey from NICU to home care.

Using natural language AI, Nestling interprets medical terminology and NICU documentation, providing parents with clear explanations. The app also offers guidance on post-discharge care including medication management, feeding schedules, and developmental milestones to watch for.
