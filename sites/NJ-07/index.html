About ChronoIQ

Every student faces the same wall: a pile of assignments, a calendar already full of commitments, and no clear path to getting everything done. ChronoIQ tears down that wall.

It connects to your Google Calendar and Google Classroom, finds your actual free time, and uses Gemini AI to break each assignment into realistic study blocks — written directly to your calendar, no manual planning needed.

Import Assignments

Pulls your active Classroom assignments into a triage queue with due dates and submission status.

AI Scheduling

Gemini 2.5 Flash analyzes your assignment details and free time windows to create an optimized plan.

Calendar Sync

Study blocks are written directly into Google Calendar, working around your existing commitments.

Progress Tracking

Check off blocks as you finish them and watch your assignment progress update in real time.

Privacy-first: ChronoIQ stores nothing on its servers. All fetched data lives in browser memory only. The only persistence is an encrypted session cookie and the calendar events ChronoIQ creates in your own Google Calendar.
