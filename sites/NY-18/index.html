About Hudson Connect

Hudson Connect helps residents of New York’s 18th Congressional District find local, accessible resources that support their daily lives. It’s designed for anyone who needs quick and reliable access to community services, including food assistance, health support, education programs, and more.

The goal of Hudson Connect is to make essential information easy to find and to strengthen the connection between community members and the services available to them.

How It Was Built

Hudson Connect was built using JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Node, and Leaflet. JavaScript and HTML create the structure and core functionality of the app, while CSS brings the visual design to life. Node powers the backend processes, and Leaflet enables the interactive map that lets users explore nearby resources. Together, these technologies create a simple, intuitive platform that helps people find the support they need.
