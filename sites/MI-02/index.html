We are a team of seniors from the cybersecurity class at the Bay Arenac ISD. For most of us, this was our first time building a website — a challenging but incredible learning experience. We're thrilled to bring PhishingHook to the showcase in D.C!

// ABOUT PHISHINGHOOK

PhishingHook is a website designed to raise cybersecurity awareness. With interactive quizzes to test your knowledge, it helps everyday users recognize phishing attempts, social engineering tactics, and common online threats.
