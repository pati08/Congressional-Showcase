About the App

What is Draftly?

Draftly is an all-in-one integrated place to keep and work with your notes, even if they're split between paper and the digital world. It does everything it can to stay out of your way so that you can work as quickly and unburdened as possible. Just take 20 seconds to scan some notes after class, and never worry about losing them again.
