HeartAngel

By Catherine Wang • CA-49 District Winner

About Me

Hi, I am Catherine, a high school student passionate about science, medicine, and technology. I enjoy combining coding and healthcare to create projects that can make a real-world impact. HeartAngel was developed from my desire to improve emergency response and help people take action during critical moments by providing real-time CPR guidance and connecting them to life-saving resources.

About the App

Giving Life A Second Chance.

HeartAngel is a mobile application designed to assist users during real-life cardiac arrest emergencies, while also offering a substantial tool for practicing CPR on a day-to-day basis with live compression feedback based on depth and speed accuracy.

This app is designed for anyone — especially bystanders who may not have formal medical training — so they can confidently take action in an emergency. It also aims to support a connected response system by linking users to nearby trained responders and AED devices during a real-life emergency. All together, HeartAngel bridges the gap of bystander unreadiness, limited access to emergency tools, and the need for faster, more coordinated responses during life-threatening situations.

HeartAngel was built using Flutter and Dart, allowing it to run across different platforms while providing a smooth and responsive user experience.
