About Me

I'm a high school student from California's 46th Congressional District passionate about making education more accessible through technology. I believe every student deserves to truly understand math not just memorize procedures.

MathFluent is my answer to the gap between getting the right answer and actually understanding why it's right.

MathFluent

MathFluent is a conceptual math learning platform that helps students understand math through visual storytelling, adaptive practice, and AI-powered tutoring.

Instead of just telling you "wrong answer," MathFluent creates real-world stories that show what your answer would look like in practice so you can see where your thinking went off track.
