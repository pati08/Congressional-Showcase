📱 The App

Mathviz has transitioned a bit, but it is primarily a website that helps students understand mathematics from a visual perspective, where anyone can simply input a basic prompt and it shows a visualization through a library known as Manim. It also has over 20k+ problems scrapped, and a custom lean interpreter for proof verification and writing along with an assistive LLM.

Built with Next and Firebase, Mathviz was previously piloted through Mathdash for a brief period, and we are currently expanding.
