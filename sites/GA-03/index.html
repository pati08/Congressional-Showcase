The App

How It Works

NxtSteps is an iOS app designed to help high school students navigate the entire path to college. From building a college list and tracking application deadlines to finding scholarships and making enrollment decisions, the app provides step-by-step guidance.

Students can organize their applications by school, track requirements and deadlines, discover scholarships that match their profile, and follow a guided roadmap through each stage of the admissions process. The app makes college planning less overwhelming and more achievable.
