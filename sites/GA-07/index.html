💙 Our Story · Why I Built SpeakEase.io

It started with a brother's voice

SpeakEase.io began as a high school student's passion project—a mission to help a beloved brother with autism find his voice. What started in a teenager's bedroom, fueled by love and late nights of coding, has grown into a tool that empowers thousands to communicate freely.

“My brother with autism taught me that everyone has something to say. Sometimes they just need the right tool to say it. That's why Speakease.io exists.”
— Hannah Kim (Founder/Developer)
