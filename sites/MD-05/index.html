The Platform

How It Works

Sokrat is a research discovery platform that helps users quickly find relevant academic materials through intuitive keyword search. Users enter search terms related to their research topic, and Sokrat returns curated results from academic sources.

The platform is designed to be accessible to everyone, from high school students working on their first research paper to professionals looking for specific studies. By streamlining the research discovery process, Sokrat removes barriers to accessing academic knowledge.
