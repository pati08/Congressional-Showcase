The App

How It Works

TroopCompanion is a comprehensive mental health app designed specifically for veterans. At its core is an AI-powered therapist that provides 24/7 support for PTSD, offering empathetic conversations and evidence-based coping strategies whenever veterans need help.

The app also includes guided breathing exercises for anxiety management, a medication tracker with reminders, and wellness monitoring tools. By combining AI therapy with practical health management features, TroopCompanion provides holistic support for veteran mental health.
